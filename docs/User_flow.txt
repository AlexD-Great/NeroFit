NeroFit User Flow:
Grab the NeroFit app and sign in with a wallet—it’s super quick thanks to Paymaster.
Connect your Fitbit or whatever you use to track runs or workouts.
Check out challenges, like “Run 5km” or “Do yoga twice this week,” from friends or even a gym nearby.
Finish your challenge. Your wearable says, “Yup, you did it!”
Score FIT tokens without paying any fees—Paymaster’s got you covered
Show off your win on a leaderboard or challenge your buddy to beat you next time.
