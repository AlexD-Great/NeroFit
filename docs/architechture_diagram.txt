        [You]

          v  
+----------------+  
| Phone App     | --> [NERO Chain Contracts] --> [Paymaster]

+----------------+  
    |         |  
    v         v  
[Fitbit APIs]  [AI (TensorFlow)]

How It Flows:  
You use the phone app, which talks to Fitbit or lets you log stuff manually. The AI suggests challenges just for you, NERO Chain handles the token stuff, and Paymaster makes sure you don’t pay fees—gyms cover it!
